Our projects

Here is a list of all the projects and policies we would like to propose and work towards if elected. If you have any questions about any of the following statements, do not hesitate to contact us on any social media platform.

Community initiatives

  • Regulated class group chats for every class. This can be done with the help of platforms like Discord
Icon

Environment

  • Gardening workshops for all students to participate in voluntarily
  • A regular composting program at the College for our own garden
  • Sustainable clothing swap/DIY thrift store to be held at least once a year
  • Starting an Ecobrick initiative
    • Icon

    Academic fairness

    • Regulated submission times for all assignments
    • Making sure learning platforms remain as uniform as possible
    • Regular surveys to be implemented to make sure that teachers are respecting their given ponderation (hours a week of homework)
    • Minimizing evaluation overlap during stressful block-end periods (ensuring deadlines are made to be as fair and reasonable as possible during this time with better communication between departments)
    Icon

    Mental health

  • Mindfulness workshops to help with anxiety, they don’t have to be very long to be effective.
  • Starting up a hotline/casual chat room for people that do not necessarily qualify for counseling services but still need a pick me up here and there
    • Icon

    Social justice

  • Mandatory sexual assault prevention seminars during synchronous classes (1 per semester)
  • Make a very visible schedule of protests that people may want to attend, just ask for your event to be included
    • Icon

    Transparency

  • An abridged version of congressional activities during meetings to be posted on social media (Instagram, Facebook, etc)
  • Biweekly general polls and idea submission to be held in order to make real student concerns be heard during our meetings
  • For anything else, open DM policy, answers to your questions in a maximum of 24-48 hours
    • Icon

    Art

  • Art fair/con to be held where students can share their artwork with others and learn how to manage a kiosk
  • Proceeds from entry for this event can be donated to the charity that students will vote for
